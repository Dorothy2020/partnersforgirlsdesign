Environmental Conservation

At PFGAF, environmental conservation plays a critical role in empowering young women and girls by fostering a sense of responsibility and leadership in protecting the environment. We teach the importance of sustainability and how everyone can contribute to the preservation of natural resources.

1. Raising Awareness About Environmental Issues

We help young women and girls understand the challenges posed by climate change, deforestation, and pollution. Through workshops, discussions, and activities, they learn how to mitigate these issues and take action to safeguard their communities.
